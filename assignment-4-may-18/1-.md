## If it is removed:

multiple functional associations cannot exist
category purpose becomes ambiguous
catalog features like search, promotions, browse root, quick add, etc. may break or behave incorrectly

So, prodCatalogCategoryTypeId defines the semantic meaning of the catalog-category relationship, not just extra metadata.


## use :
Without PCCT_BROWSE_ROOT:

system may not know which category is the entry point of the catalog
category navigation hierarchy can become unclear
storefront browse structure may not render correctly

So in OMS/OFBiz, it is mainly used for:

storefront catalog navigation
category tree building
browse flow initialization
top-level category rendering
